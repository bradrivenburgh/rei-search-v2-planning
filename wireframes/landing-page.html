About rei-search

Real estate investment decisions should always begin with proper due diligence. This app is meant to be used by investors to discover and evaluate key statistics about the population, economy, and quality of life characteristics in and around municipalities in the United States.

Get marketplace statistics

The app produces statistical data primarily on two geographic levels:

  • Metropolitan Statistical Areas: geographic regions centered around areas of high population density (i.e., big cities) with integrated economies
  • Census Tracts: a small, focused geographic unit used for statistical purposes by the US Census Bureau, often correlating closely to city town boundaries.

How to get started

To get started searching for your next investment property:

  1. Navigate to the main page by clicking the "X" icon at the top of this page
  2. Create an account to save select search results or proceed as a visitor and create an account at another time
  3. Search geographies with a zip code or city and state, and review the data to narrow down where you will invest next.
