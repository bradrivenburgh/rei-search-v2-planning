About rei-search

Real estate investment decisions should always begin with proper due diligence. This app is meant to be used by investors to discover and evaluate key statistics about the population and economy of geographical regions in the United States on two levels:

  • Metropolitan Statistical Areas: geographic regions centered around areas of high population density (i.e., big cities) with integrated economies
  • Census Tracts: a small, focused geographic unit used for statistical purposes by the US Census Bureau, often correlating closely to city town boundaries.
